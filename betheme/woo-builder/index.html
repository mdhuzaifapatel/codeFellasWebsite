attribute swatches

Create custom attribute swatches and set them individually
for single products whenever you need them.

Types of attributes

Select

Attribute with almost unlimited possibilities whenever needed

Color

Use included colorpicker to define colors you exactly need

Image

Any variations you want based on the pictures added

Label

Ideal attribute for sizes, weight and much more...

Drop-down Login

Let your customers login into the account directly from header and manage their account at click.

  • No need to go to the login page
  • Quick access to Orders, Downloads, Addresses and much more

Wishlist

Allow visitors to add products to the wishlist so they can manage them and purchase later.

  1. Add to the Wishlist from single product
  2. Add to the Wishlist directly from the list
  3. Manage added products on the Wishlist page

Eye-catching Side Cart

A great alternative to the default cart, which will make shopping for your customers much easier.

Perfectly visible photos of the products with its attributes, clear shipping costs, calculating the contents of the basket live and much more...

Catalogue mode

This optional feature let you easily remove Add to Cart button with a click in order to exclude the possibility of purchasing the product. Perfect solution if you want to present products only still using the WooCommerce.

Product gallery
styles

Choose between as many as 11 gorgeous gallery styles and present product photos exactly as you want.

  • Outside & Overlay
  • Left / Center / Right
  • Top / Middle / Bottom

Quick preview

Preview the product without leaving the current page. Let customers preview and add items to their cart from within the lightbox or let them interact with product before adding it to their cart or wishlist. This feature significantly increase the chance of a sale!

Off-canvas
sidebar

Whenever you need product filters or just want to show something you do not have space on the product list page, off-canvas sidebar comes in handy.

  • Display categories & sub-categories
  • Filter products by custom attribute swatches
  • Add images, widgets and much more...

View options bar

Let your visitors change the number of products per page, layout & display filters, all without reloading the page. Display all options at once or just one of them, exactly as you need.

Bottom
sticky menu

Let your potential customers have at their fingertips what is most important in the store, i.e. product list, cart, wishlist and account details.

This bar will always be sticky to the bottom on mobile devices and will follow the user while browsing the products in the store.

Meet the Woo Elements

For the need to build Shop and Single Product templates, we created 16 elements specially adapted for the WooCommerce. The configurations in which they can be used give almost unlimited possibilities of creation.

  • Shop categories

    Display categories & customize its appearance

  • Shop products

    List products according to your custom settings

  • Shop title

    Set title & customize its alignment, tag or size

  • Product title

    Add anywhere you want & customize its settings

  • Product images

    Drag & Drop gallery wherever you like and customize

  • Product price

    Set individual style for the regular & sale price

  • Add to cart

    Allow buyers to select the quantity and add to the cart

  • Product reviews

    Put reviews wherever you like in the size you need

  • Product rating

    This Element convert customer interest into sales

  • Product stock

    Great information about the stock availability

  • Product meta

    Change the style for meta tags like category as you like

  • Short description

    Provide your visitor quick information about the product

  • Product content

    Detailed description and marketing about the product

  • Additional information

    Provide detailed information for your potential customers

  • Product related

    Display products that share the same tags or categories

  • Product upsells

    Necessary in every store to increase sales
