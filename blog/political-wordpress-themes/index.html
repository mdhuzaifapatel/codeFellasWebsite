Nominee

 

Nominee is a tool for building websites for politicians. It caters to those from any level of government, from local politicians to party leaders.

Nominee features everything that a good political WordPress theme should. It includes four different layouts that are especially suited for political websites.

The main demo has been designed for a political candidate’s website. The other layouts are more focused on election campaigns.

One of these layouts has a countdown clock that counts down to the election day. It also has a section with information about the candidate and an online donation form to raise funds for the political campaign.

Ridhi

 

Ridhi offers a Pro plan for their WordPress themes, as well as a pretty complete free version. Their themes can help users to build responsive political websites.

They also work well for small business organization sites. Political consultants, mentors, and speakers especially, are the target market of Ridhi.

Free Ridhi comes with one header and one portfolio layout, as well as ten homepage sections. The Pro version contains three headers, three portfolio layouts, and twenty homepage sections.

Alone

 

Alone is for politicians, campaigns, political candidates, and organizations. Alone offers a total of 15 website demos, 2 of which are specifically for political purposes.

These 2 options are perfect for making a website that conveys the right message and feeling. They create a site that catches the interest of the viewer.

This political WordPress theme is optimized for search engines. This ensures that people will find their way to the website through Google easier.

Its templates are fully responsive and retina ready. They invite creativity and ensure that the result will be something unique.

CityGov

 

CityGov is a political WordPress theme aimed at municipalities. The design is slick and responsive, allowing visitors to view the website on any device.

The CityGov theme has 70 adjustable layout parameters and is fully customizable. It supports Elementor Page Builder, which makes designing and customizing pages even simpler.

The advanced theme administrator panel makes site management an easy task.

Miata

 

This political WordPress theme is for designing beautiful campaign sites. These include campaign sites for candidates, debates, donations, and elections.

Miata offers various homepage layouts with many customizable elements. These options give users control over the appearance of the website, according to their needs and preferences.

Miata supports CSS3, HTML5, WooCommerce, and WPML, within the WordPress framework. It also includes unlimited plugins, widgets, and colors.

Miata offers excellent dedicated support.

Politono

 

The Politono WordPress theme has everything a political site builder needs. The prebuilt demos have several customizable options to make a stylish political website.

With all the flexibility that this political WordPress theme contains, the user is not limited to just the default page.

Politono is built around the Elementor plugin. This means that the user can make changes and edits using a visual drag and drop interface.

The WordPress Customizer allows the user to make even more adjustments to the site’s appearance.

VoteStart

 

With this political WordPress theme, those in politics will leave a lasting impression.

Users can present anything related to politics with VoteStart, from campaigns to activism. It does not matter the site is for presenting a person, a team, or a campaign.

The thing that sets VoteStart apart from the rest is the set of event layouts. With this, a designer can present everything related to one single event or a series of events.

It will keep all the information together so that people will know where and when to go to support. On top of that, VoteStart offers many elements related to causes and donations.

Candidate

 

Candidate is a WordPress theme for political and nonprofit organizations. This adaptable WordPress theme raises awareness for a specific project.

Although churches and NPOs can benefit from Candidate, it is especially suited for political causes. It offers four different political demos, with twelve demo websites.

These demos make it quick and easy to set up a political website. Each demo offers different features.

These include a homepage slideshow, a donation widget, an events feed, social media content, or a news feed.

Campaign

 

Campaign is one of the best political WordPress themes for campaigns. The features that it provides are very effective and customizable.

Its focus is on providing flexibility to the user and this is the basis of its good reputation.

The Campaign theme includes 2 body styles, 4 color schemes, 2 content layouts, and 6 background patterns. This gives users many different design options, either for a single category or for all pages.

Widgets for the home page or footer add extra functionality to the political website.

VoxPopuli

 

VoxPopuli offers original typography and special page transitions. These make VoxPopuli unique, but it also offers an array of adjustable options.

It works well with different browsers and platforms. Premium subscription adds color options and tones for extra customization.

VoxPopuli features three demos that are for use on the inner pages. The demos offer customization by changing colors, fonts, icons, media, and shapes.

Politix

 

Promoting campaigns, election candidates, and other political causes are among its applications. It is powerful and flexible.

The Politix package includes a toolbox that makes building a political website easy.

In the package are four homepage designs with various inner page layouts. It is a matter of mixing and matching the elements that Politix provides to make an engaging site.

Frontrunner

 

Frontrunner is a multi-purpose political WordPress theme. It includes various demos that contain something for any politics-related website.

The Frontrunner demos help users to get started quickly. It has demos for campaigns for congressional candidates, city councilors, and mayoral candidates.

It even has demos for runners for school and college elections.

The template for landing pages is especially suited for campaigns, inviting more leads. It also has a donation feature to facilitate the raising of campaign funds.

Dream City

 

Dream City helps cities and municipalities present their town to tourists and visitors. It also helps local politicians and governments to present themselves to their citizens.

The user can import ready-made homepage layouts. These layouts help to get started in building a website or portal packed with features.

The Dream City themes support CSS3, HTML5, and WPML and it has a custom admin panel. Other features and options include:

  • Events Calendar
  • Google Fonts
  • Plugin friendly
  • SEO options
  • Shortcodes
  • Sliders
  • Widgets
  • WooCommerce

Election

 

Election promises that it will help the user win the race. It aims at politicians, law businesses, campaigns, and similar organizations.

The Election theme delivers appealing and impressive websites. The code is of excellent quality and has comprehensive and practical documentation.

Additional support is available. The retina-ready graphics and style options look good on computers and mobile devices.

It is compatible with CSS3 and HTML5.

Right Way

 

Right Way is a WordPress theme made for political websites. It has a modern and stylish look.

This WordPress theme contains many features and adjustable options. For example, there are 11 different homepage layouts and 5 different header styles.

Coding is in CSS3 and HTML5 format and it makes use of the newest technologies. These factors make Right Way completely responsive.

It offers various slider creators such as Swiper Slider, Revolution Slider, and FlexSlider. Using Right Way will result in beautiful and original websites.

Right Way is also made for creating campaign portfolio pages. There are 15 variants of 3 portfolio styles, classic, grid, and masonry.

Politica

 

Politica is the next political WordPress theme. It contains features that suit websites for political candidates, campaigns, and donations.

The well-written HTML5 and CSS3 code makes it suitable for use on any device.

The importer feature imports and installs one of the various demos onto a website. Politica also makes use of the Visual Composer plugin.

This allows users to drag and drop items to build and customize their websites. Politica is compatible with WooCommerce and comes with a free donation management plugin.

The user can choose from three different headers and a couple of different footers.

Right Candidate

 

Right Candidate is for websites for political leaders, candidates, campaigns, and parties. It includes e-commerce possibilities, and donation and event features.

There are two pre-built homepages and various inside pages.

The donations feature is available through the free Custom Donations plugin. Website visitors can make donations online, which can be used to finance one or more campaigns.

Politics

 

Politics is a free political WordPress theme. Politicians, campaigns, governments, and political causes can make use of it.

It features a default homepage layout. The user can use this and adapt it to meet their needs and preferences.

The Politics theme is very flexible and it allows the user to move and hide items at will.

Besides the default homepage, Politics has a couple of templates for building a complete website. There are blog templates, and ready-made contact and donations pages.

MCKinney's Politics

 

MCKinney’s Politics is a modern and stylish WordPress theme for political purposes. It is great for politicians, activists, campaigns, political parties, and social movements.

The WordPress theme targets any political topics, such as human rights, volunteers, and fundraising.

It is useful too for political candidates, political reviews, and non-profit organizations. This political theme includes:

  • Events management
  • Nine political news and gallery layouts
  • WooCommerce store
  • Much more

Politician

 

Politician is one of the many political WordPress themes that are available. It aims at politicians and has a modern look, making use of current technology.

Politician is a complete tool for creating and customizing a website’s layout. The coding uses valid HTML5 and Bootstrap 3.x.

It offers several useful features, including:

  • Custom Widgets
  • Backend Page Composer
  • Revolution Slider
  • Sample Data One Click Tool
  • WooCommerce integration

Coup

 

The Coup WordPress theme is best for political campaigns, charities, non-governmental organizations, and churches. Coup provides the features that are necessary for furthering a political cause.

This political theme has an integrated donation form for receiving financial support.

Potisen

 

Potisen focuses on creating sites for promoting election campaigns. This political WordPress theme offers four demos with differently designed homepages.

The variety allows the user to create a WordPress site that meets their needs.

The design includes full-screen sliders to show photos or videos of the candidates. The sliders are also useful for sharing political campaign statistics, like the number of followers.

The donation widget makes it possible to receive campaign donations online.

PoliticalWP

 

This smart-looking theme is perfect for political websites, like elections and donations. The additional features make it a good choice.

There is a one-click importer included, so it is not necessary to build the website from scratch. PoliticalWP includes some demo sites.

Social Activity

 

With a special event feature and a donation option, Social Activity is a good solution for promoting a political cause. It is ready for use by political parties, NGOs, social movements, and fundraisers.

The Social Activity themes are built using the latest web standards. The developers used valid CSS3 and HTML5 code for developing the pages.

It allows the use of plugins and the possibilities of designs are endless. The clean code makes the website responsive and retina-ready.

CityZen

 

CityZen aims to fill the needs of non-profit organizations. It’s also suitable for political candidates, ministers, parties, municipalities, and government offices.

CityZen is completely responsive, with full optimization for use on smartphones and tablets. It is a political theme with a neat, modern look.

Politico

 

This top feature-packed theme is most suited for political campaign sites. It invites the public to visit its users’ websites.

It is an excellent tool for promoting a cause or political party.

Politico comes with two slideshow options: Nivo Slider and Dual Slider. A Website with a slideshow is sure to attract the attention of the visitors and promote the candidate.

Some other notable features are:

  • Responsive layout
  • Retina-ready
  • Transition ready
  • WP theme customizer

Baklon

 

Those that are planning to start a political website should consider Baklon for their website theme. It is an election and a political WordPress theme.

Baklon is fitting for a political leader, candidate, party, or related organization.

It integrates with the Event Calendar through a plugin. Through this feature, it can display upcoming campaign events on the website.

Baklon also supports the Donation plugin. This enables visitors to make online donations to the campaign or political party.

The user can choose from various pre-built layouts to build their website. In addition to the plugins mentioned above, Baklon supports Revolution Slider and Woocommerce.

Ending thoughts on political WordPress themes

Being a politician is a big responsibility, one of the biggest imaginable. It takes an excellent education, the right personality, credibility, trustworthiness, and a vision.

But it is also vital to think about how to reach the public and create a network of supporters. A politician needs to be able to communicate with them at any place and at any time.

Political WordPress themes help to create an attractive website that conveys the right message.

The right WordPress theme should be modern in appearance and offer compatibility with necessary plugins. The ease of installation and customizability are also important factors to consider.
