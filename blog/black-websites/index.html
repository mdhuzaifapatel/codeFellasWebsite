Black websites are a very popular web design option. It’s a unique type of design that uses light lettering, or contrast lettering, to obtain the desired effects. This effect makes the text stand out and grabs the users’ attention. This is considered the reversal of traditional black and white design. It works very well for some brands in varying conditions.

Black is rarely used as the primary color in web design. However, when used correctly, it can create stunning visual and design effects. Black websites help the visitor focus on the artwork and content of the website, and black can be a great branding tool.

Why Go With Black Design?

Websites that choose a black design will usually have a more serious tone. It adds a level of sophistication as well as a classic feel. The black design is fantastic for bringing the attention of the user on specific parts of the website, or content that is highly important.

Black is a widely used color option for websites, and they are quite common, but what effect does black have on a website?

  • It gives an appearance of elegance and sophistication.
  • It adds an element of mystery to the design.
  • Black is an amazing color for creating contrasts.
  • There’s a visual hierarchy created on the website.
  • It allows you to effectively present the content on your website.

Black also doesn’t strain your eyes as much as white, so it’s easier to scroll through the website for prolonged periods. That’s especially crucial when we browse at night. That’s also a reason why many popular websites, such as Twitter or Youtube, offer an option to switch to a black theme, allowing for prolonged use without too much strain.

This article will explore the best black websites on the internet today, giving you insight and inspiration for creating a black website.
