Kvell offers an array of multipurpose themes. A great option for freelancers, digital, and marketing agencies, as well as creative workers in general.

Owly

 

Schools, tutors, and teachers have not been left out. Owly implemented its creative WordPress themes with attractive and captivating options.

Gillion

 

This WordPress theme called Gillion helps make a good first impression that will charm your website visitors. The integrated Slider Revolution plugin means a boundlessly creative slideshow. The premium plugin option is free of charge.

Jack Well

 

Jack Well is a WordPress theme that comes with the Slider Revolution premium plugin. The plugin has a mobile-friendly function. This guarantees an efficient representation of important information no matter what kind of device is used.

The7

 

Seamless and full integration with Ultimate Addons LDT and WPBakery Page Builder is seen in The7. Over 1000 theme options and an extensive design library allow creativeness. The design wizard can give your website a boutique feel.

X Theme

 

X Theme has four pre-built starter themes, so any design look or layout is available.

X Theme also created the Cornerstone page builder. The purpose was to provide users with the freedom and flexibility to customize front-end design.

Slider Revolution, LayerSlider, and Soliloquy are all incorporated within X Theme. It also contains a free Smart Slider 3 plugin, providing full functionality. Input the shortcode provided by Smart Slider 3 using Cornerstone to display the slider.

Soledad

 

Soledad helps bloggers customize their posts, offering over 250 demo options. It also comes with six header styles, five article layouts, and two menu styles.

Understanding that blogs often need updates, Soledad includes a WordPress customizer. This customizer enables the user to manage and maintain a blog in a convenient way.

Peggi

 

Peggi is a multipurpose WordPress theme for businesses geared towards children and their needs. Kindergartens, babysitters, and daycare centers are among its customers.

It offers many features, layouts, and the Slider Revolution plugin. It presents all the elements necessary for building comprehensive and engaging content.

Peggi offers modern page template designs, animations, and transition effects. These can be implemented in customizable slides and slide position grids. With these elements, a website will make a lasting impression.

Jevelin

 

One of the appealing features of this WordPress theme is that programming experience is not needed. The many customizing features can be used directly and intuitively.

Javelin combines the Unyson framework with a drag-and-drop page builder. This option offers design flexibility with many pre-built templates and layouts.

It ensures a clean and fresh look by providing many color options and parallax effects for backgrounds and videos.

With Slider Revolution photos and videos can be customized. It provides widgets for various social media platforms. This WordPress theme is sure to help draw attention to your site.

This multipurpose WordPress theme helps businesses compile professional content for their websites, without the need to invest in a high-end set of templates.

The homepage layout has been designed in such a way as to present the key elements of a business. It highlights services, mission statements, and other pertinent information in a sleek and elegant way.

It integrates with the popular Slider Revolution plugin. Thus, it allows you to incorporate appealing slideshows into a website. This feature ensures that a target audience is reached through video, audio, images, and text.

Ozisti

 

This sleek and modern multipurpose WooCommerce WordPress theme has state-of-the-art Augmented reality (AR) integration. AR is gaining increasing popularity with its innovative and groundbreaking technology.

Ozisti has made it possible to build more than a WooCommerce store and enables users to create online stores that suit a variety of different domains.

Investing in this leading-edge technology may be exactly what your business needs for success. With its integrated theme from Slider Revolution, you will have the added feature of adding eye-catching sliders.

Avada

 

This mobile-friendly theme is very popular because of its compatibility with various leading plugins. Avada works with WPML, Yoast, W3TC, Contact Form 7, JetPack, Gravity Forms, Slider Revolution, and more.

The theme is diverse, making it easy to adapt content based on customers’ needs, as well as maintaining aesthetic appeal.

It provides a live demo, endless color choices, and personalized feature settings. Avada premium theme helps to achieve an enticing customized page, with a straightforward interface.

Crane

 

This WordPress theme has been created with online businesses and portfolio building in mind. Crane offers a wide array of demos with customizable options, as well as a drag-and-drop page building feature.

Moreover, it offers pre-built theme options. The amazing retina-ready choices result in striking visual elements.

Crane partners with Slider Revolution and Layer Slider plugins for visuals. As a result of that, it means it offers parallax backgrounds, sidebars, and headings, in a convenient cross-browser compatible format.

Shopkeeper

 

Shopkeeper includes sharp images, fully responsive, and cross-browser compatibility. Shopkeeper is a user-friendly WordPress e-commerce platform.

Designed to catch the eye of potential customers, the homepage consists of one large attention-grabbing slider. To make the slider, Shopkeeper employs the Slider Revolution plugin.

Other plugins can be installed as well, including the “Shopkeeper Portfolio Addon”.

Users do not need to have extensive knowledge of programming to benefit from the features provided. There is a wide array of pre-built layouts, as well as the option to build one from scratch.

Ottar

 

Ottar is a professional portfolio building theme providing a wide range of creative layouts and columns. With the help of Slider Revolution, split slider templates are among its possibilities.

It provides practical and aesthetic page options, as well as blog and shop templates that can be supported with slides.

Ottar also offers some amazing presentation options. It contains three header themes, a portfolio carousel, testimonials, and tooltip text. These elements add value to a website or portfolio.

Romada

 

This theme aims at advancing creative agencies and modern startups. It supports many plugins such as WPBakery page builder, WooCommerce, and Slider Revolution. Additionally, this WordPress theme has many unique and creative elements.

Users can choose between two pre-built homepage designs, and then mix and match various inner page designs from there.

Bridge

 

Using Elementor and WPBakery, this theme provides over 500 unique examples for business, website, or blog content. With Slider Revolution, video sliders, carousel sliders, and 3D animated photo sliders are within reach.

Customizable designs and layouts are presented with innovative and authentic insights. Parallax sections, multiple logo versions, customized typography, and more, belong to its possibilities. The user-friendly admin interface also ensures a straightforward way to access and import content.

Monyxi

 

This multipurpose cryptocurrency WordPress theme is powerful, modern, and bold. This makes Monyxi perfect for financial blogs, and cryptocurrency information in general.

The theme offers many plugins, such as Slider Revolution and Events Calendar. These can be used to organize events, schedules, and to keep clients up-to-date on business workshops.

This WordPress theme comes with a lot of financial documentation. It is thus a good source of financial, cryptocurrency, and bookkeeping information.

Orion

 

This multipurpose WordPress theme has many selections for demos, homepages, headers, and footers. This versatility offers unlimited design options. Orion has as its main goal to become the leading theme for portfolio agencies and freelancers.

Orion has two principal categories: Home Minimal and Home Creative. Supporting AJAX and using advanced options for fonts, and has portfolio integration with Masonry, Title Grid, and more.

Using Retina Ready has made it adaptable to any device, including smartphones. Design features include Parallax for backgrounds and MegaMenu for the main posting.

Included with the package are custom page templates, Slider Revolution, and Visual Composer features at no extra cost.

Unicon

 

The advanced features of this WordPress theme ensure that visitors to your site will have a unique navigation experience. The premium design-focused theme can enhance the appeal of any business website.

It is suitable for any business needs, including e-commerce, design, corporate.

Uncode

 

The last of these WordPress themes with Slider Revolution is Uncode. This theme design is based on the popular page builder versions to create a functional multipurpose WordPress theme. It combines its eCommerce themes with over 400 templates for art and music content.
